About Municipal Waste Management System

Our platform is designed to bridge the gap between citizens and municipal authorities, ensuring swift action on waste management complaints. By empowering people to report issues directly, we foster cleaner environments and healthier communities.

Aim:

  • To create a transparent and efficient channel for reporting waste-related problems.
  • To assist municipalities in maintaining hygiene and environmental standards.

Objectives:

  • Enable citizens to quickly lodge complaints regarding garbage overflow, illegal dumping, missed pickups, etc.
  • Help municipal staff track, manage, and resolve complaints in a timely manner.
  • Promote civic responsibility and public participation in keeping cities clean.
  • Enhance communication between local authorities and residents for better urban living.
